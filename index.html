Who We Are

Building a Healthy,
Green World Together

Green Eco-Life Society (GLS) is a Kenyan-based non-profit organisation dedicated to promoting environmental conservation, sustainable development, and eco-friendly lifestyles. We implement practical community development programs that bridge conservation and livelihoods.

What We Do

Our Five Core
Programs

A holistic sustainability model connecting environmental protection, economic empowerment, and healthy living.

01 / 05
🌳
Tree Planting & Climate Restoration
Restoring Kenya's forest cover through community-led afforestation, school green clubs, and corporate CSR partnerships. Natural conservation through tree cover.
  • Seedling nurseries & afforestation campaigns
  • School-based green clubs nationwide
  • Corporate CSR tree-planting packages
  • Agroforestry & community forestry training
02 / 05
♻️
Waste Management & Recycling
Turning Kenya's waste crisis into a circular economy opportunity through education, recycling training, and composting demonstrations.
  • Household & institutional waste education
  • Community cleanups
  • Recycling & upcycling training
  • Waste-to-energy demonstrations
03 / 05
☀️
Green Energy — Solar Solutions
Bringing affordable, clean solar energy to Kenyan households and institutions, replacing kerosene and reducing deforestation from firewood use.
  • Solar training & installation support
  • Clean cookstove dissemination
  • Energy conservation sensitization
04 / 05
🌾
Organic Farming & Natural Herbs
Promoting chemical-free farming, herbal medicine, and organic food systems that improve soil health, nutrition, and community livelihoods.
  • Demonstration farms in 47 counties
  • Herbal medicine awareness & promotion
  • Organic inputs training
05 / 05
💧
Sustainable Development Projects
Building resilient communities through water projects, eco-friendly infrastructure, and environmental livelihood programmes.
  • Water projects & rainwater harvesting
  • Eco-friendly infrastructure
  • Environmental livelihood programs
